Smoothie

Refreshing strawberry smoothie, perfect for breakfast or as a midday snack.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Strawberries
  • Frozen Bananas
  • Dates
  • Milk
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Place strawberries, bananas, dates, yogurt, and milk in a blender and cover.
  2. Turn blender on low until ingredients begin to combine and then gradually increase the blender speed until it's on high.
  3. Blend ingredients until completely combined then turn blender off.
  4. Pour contents into cup(s) and enjoy cold!
