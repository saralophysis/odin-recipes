Pizza

Fresh, homemade cheese pizza with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Favorite Toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F OR preheat your pizza oven.
  2. Place pizza crust on a round baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce around the crust, leaving an inch of space on the outer edge.
  4. Sprinkle a thick layer of cheese on top of the tomato sauce.
  5. (optional) Sprinkle your favorite toppings over the cheese layer.
  6. Place pizza in the oven and bake until the cheese begins to bubble and the crust begins to turn golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and slice pizza.
  8. Enjoy!
