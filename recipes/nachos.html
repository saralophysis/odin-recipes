Nachos

Nachos made with cheese, chili, pico de gallo, jalapenos, guacamole, and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Chili
  • Pico De Gallo
  • Jalapenos
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Arrange tortilla chips in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. Top chips with half of the cheese and half of the chili.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 on top of the first layer of chips.
  4. Set oven to Broil on High and place baking sheet with chips and toppings into the oven. Allow to crisp up for 5 minutes or until chips begin to turn brown.
  5. Pull sheet out of the oven carefully and dish chips with toppings onto separate plates.
  6. Top the chips with pico de gallo, jalapenos, guacamole, sour cream, and cilantro.
  7. Enjoy!
